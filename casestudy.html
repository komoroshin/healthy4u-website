A Growing Clinic Struggling with Paperwork

GavCure Clinic was experiencing rapid growth, but their success came with mounting operational challenges:

  • Unstructured intake using paper forms causing inconsistent records
  • Physicians spending 15+ minutes per appointment on history gathering
  • Patient dissatisfaction and drop-off after initial visits
  • Growth limitations without additional staff

The clinic needed a solution that could standardize care delivery while reducing administrative burden on physicians.

Implementing Healthy4U

A comprehensive platform that transformed how GavCure delivers care

AI-Powered Intake

Patients complete structured questionnaires via mobile app; AI organizes responses into medical overview

Ambient Documentation

AI-Scribe transcribes consultations and generates clinical notes for physician review

Unified Patient Records

Consolidated health data (history, vitals, previous visits, lab results)

Continuous Monitoring

Between-visit symptom and vital tracking with physician alerts

Transformed Operations, Better Care

The implementation of Healthy4U delivered measurable improvements across all aspects of clinic operations:

  • Standardized diagnostic workflows ensuring consistent care quality
  • Reduced patient drop-off post-visit
  • Predictable patient experience without repetitive questioning
  • Enhanced decision-making with complete patient context
